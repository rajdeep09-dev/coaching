## 💡 Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific about animations**: "Add a spring animation with stiffness 100"
2. **Reference real sites**: "Like the Apple product page"
3. **Mention colors**: "Dark theme with purple accents"
4. **Specify responsive**: "Mobile-first, stack on mobile"
5. **Include interactions**: "Hover scales 1.05 with shadow"
